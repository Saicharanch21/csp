About Us

This Website is developed as mini project which is part of Community Service Project(CSP).

Community Service Project is an experiential learning strategy that integrates meaningful community service with instruction, participation, learning and community development.It involves students in community development and service activities and applies the experience to personal and academic development.Community Service Project is meant to link the community with the college for mutual benefit. The community will be benefited with the focused contribution of the college students for the village/ local development. The college finds an opportunity to develop social sensibility and responsibility among students and also emerge as a socially responsible institution.
